FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about CVNova.

Is CVNova really free?
+
Yes, CVNova is 100% free — no hidden fees, no premium plans, no watermarks. Build and download unlimited CVs at no cost.
Do I need to create an account?
+
No account or sign-up required. Just open CVNova, fill in your information, and download your CV instantly.
What is ATS and why does it matter?
+
ATS (Applicant Tracking System) is software used by employers to filter resumes. Our templates are designed to pass these systems, ensuring your CV reaches human recruiters.
Is my data safe?
+
Absolutely. All processing happens locally in your browser. Your personal information is never sent to any server — your data stays on your device.
Can I use CVNova on my phone?
+
Yes! CVNova is fully responsive and works on all devices — smartphones, tablets, and desktops. Build your CV on the go.
